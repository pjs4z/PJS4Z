# Mechanistic Worldview

**Definition:** A dominant cultural paradigm and belief system asserting that the universe operates as a random, indifferent accident governed exclusively by observable physical phenomena and biological chemistry.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
The [[Mechanistic Worldview]] acts as a powerful inhibitory force in psychological development. It fundamentally inhibits the adoption and effective practice of [[Active Love]] by invalidating the existence of [[Higher Forces]]. Consequently, it dramatically increases vulnerability to [[The Maze]], as it strips life events of deeper meaning and fosters a defensive, isolated posture. This dynamic is the engine of the [[Rationalist Resentment Trap]].
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Sociocultural conditioning
- **Downstream (Drives):** [[The Maze]] (increases), [[Active Love]] (inhibits)
