# Psychic Entropy

**Definition:** A state of disorder in consciousness caused by the intrusion of unfulfilled bodily needs, external fears, or everyday anxieties. It disrupts the smooth allocation of psychic energy and diminishes the subjective quality of experience.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
[[Psychic Entropy]] represents the baseline decay of unmanaged human consciousness, manifesting as either anxiety or boredom depending on the stimuli.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Increased by engaging in meaningless [[Exotelic Activity]] or pursuing external [[Symbols of Happiness]].
- **Downstream (Drives):** Actively inhibits the formation of [[Flow]]. 
- **Mitigation Mechanisms:** Temporarily decreased by [[Pleasure]] (Homeostatic Restoration loop) or minor, patterned actions known as [[Microflow]]. Completely eradicated during deep [[Flow]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
[[Psychic Entropy]] acts as an automatic Motivation driver—individuals subconsciously seek to avoid the discomfort of inner disorder.
- **Capability Leverage:** Teach individuals specific, non-disruptive private rituals (mental sequencing, breathing) to actively manage [[Psychic Entropy]] via [[Microflow]] when full engagement in an [[Autotelic Activity]] is impossible.
- **Opportunity Leverage:** Embed unobtrusive, patterned affordances into high-boredom environments to safely bleed off [[Psychic Entropy]] without derailing primary tasks.
