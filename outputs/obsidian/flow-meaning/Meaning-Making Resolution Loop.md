# Meaning-Making Resolution Loop

**Definition:** A reinforcing feedback cycle wherein a core purpose establishes overarching rules for action, which catalyzes sustained effort, leading to a state of optimal experience that ultimately reinforces the individual's commitment to the original purpose.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
This is a **Reinforcing Loop** that builds resilience and meaning over a lifetime. 
1. A [[Life Theme]] (Stock) establishes overarching rules and purpose.
2. This catalyzes [[Goal Resolution]] (Flow), the active translation of intent into persistent action despite opposition.
3. Sustained effort via [[Goal Resolution]] unlocks a [[Unified Flow Experience]] (Flow).
4. Experiencing this highly enjoyable, unified state reinforces the inherent value of the original goals, thereby deepening the individual's commitment to the [[Life Theme]].

- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Life Theme]], [[Goal Resolution]]
- **Downstream (Drives):** [[Inner Harmony]]

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
To maintain the momentum of this loop, environmental constraints are necessary.
- **Leverage Point:** Engineer "Choice Architecture" constraints. Design daily living or working environments that artificially limit extraneous options and mobility. By reducing the "embarrassment of riches" (excessive choices), you prevent decision fatigue, protecting the psychic energy required for unwavering [[Goal Resolution]].
