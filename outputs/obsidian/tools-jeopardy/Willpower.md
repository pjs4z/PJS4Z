# Willpower

**Definition:** A self-generated internal higher force that enables an individual to take necessary action despite demoralization, fear, or the temptation of immediate gratification. Unlike other higher forces considered 'gifts', this capacity must be actively forged by the individual in moments of darkness.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
[[Willpower]] is the central resource stock in the psychological system, functioning as the vital "spark" that pushes back against systemic entropy. It is the key regulating variable in the [[The Spark in the Darkness]] balancing loop.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Directly generated by the intense urgency of [[Jeopardy]].
- **Downstream (Drives):** Increases the prevalence of [[The Creator]] archetype, while strongly inhibiting both [[Demoralization]] and [[The Consumer]] behaviors.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
[[Willpower]] represents a core Psychological Capability. It is the critical threshold resource required to endure the discomfort of continuous effort and to reject the ubiquitous, low-friction distractions provided by [[Consumerism]]. 
- **Leverage Point:** Reframing failure is essential. Instead of viewing failure as a permanent state, treat it as the necessary contextual "darkness" required to practice generating the "spark" of [[Willpower]]. This shifts the user from a fragile state to an anti-fragile, continuous growth model.
