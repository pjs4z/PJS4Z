# Demoralization

**Definition:** A debilitating psychological event and subsequent state of profound defeat that inevitably occurs when the false hope of exoneration is shattered. It represents a loss of future potential where an individual becomes too paralyzed to make further efforts.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
[[Demoralization]] represents a systemic failure state, but paradoxically also provides the necessary environmental precondition (the "darkness") for systemic regeneration via [[The Spark in the Darkness]] loop.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** The shattering of the [[Exoneration]] fantasy.
- **Downstream (Drives):** Severely decreases existing [[Willpower]], but simultaneously serves as the critical cue/catalyst to invoke [[Jeopardy]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
When [[Demoralization]] occurs, an individual's Psychological Capability and Motivation drop to near zero. They lose the emotional regulation skills needed to avoid avoidant behaviors.
- **Leverage Point:** Because Motivation is depleted, interventions must rely on highly accessible, low-friction Automatic triggers. Training the individual to recognize the onset of [[Demoralization]] (e.g., the urge to stay in bed, severe apathy) as an automatic, non-negotiable cue to trigger the [[Jeopardy]] visualization can bypass the need for baseline motivation.
