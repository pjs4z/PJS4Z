# Active Love

**Definition:** A conscious, deliberate psychological tool and protocol used to intentionally generate and direct love toward an antagonist. It requires active psychological effort to synchronize the user with the macro-force of [[Outflow]], thereby liberating them from [[The Maze]].

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
[[Active Love]] is the primary intervention mechanism designed to short-circuit the **Active Love Resistance** feedback loop. While the intense anger of [[The Maze]] strongly inhibits the initiation of this tool, disciplined execution of [[Active Love]] forcefully overrides that resistance. 

The protocol is an Occurrent process composed of three sequential temporal parts:
1. **[[Concentration]]:** Visualizing infinite love and gathering it in the chest.
2. **[[Transmission]]:** Projecting this concentrated love outward toward the target.
3. **[[Penetration]]:** The subjective sensation of the transmitted love entering the target's solar plexus, creating oneness.

- **Upstream (Driven by):** Inhibited by [[The Maze]]. Requires conscious, disciplined effort to initiate.
- **Downstream (Drives):** Increases [[Outflow]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
Executing [[Active Love]] requires precise behavioral engineering to overcome the brain's natural threat responses.

- **Capability:** Requires the cognitive ability to remember and sequence [[Concentration]], [[Transmission]], and [[Penetration]]. It also demands the mental bandwidth to visualize projecting love while actively experiencing emotional turbulence.
- **Opportunity:** Triggered by specific cues—an immediate interpersonal offense, rumination on a past wrong, or the anticipation of encountering a difficult person.
- **Motivation:** Relies heavily on *Reflective Motivation*. The user must act out of conscious self-interest (the desire to avoid stagnation) rather than moral obligation. 

**Leverage Points for Behavioral Adoption:**
- *De-identification (Capability):* If the user cannot overcome rage during [[Transmission]], they should visualize the target without a face, aiming energy solely at the solar plexus to facilitate successful [[Penetration]].
- *Self-Interest Framing (Motivation):* Reframe the tool from "doing the right thing" to an act of pragmatic self-preservation to retain personal power.
- *Inoculation Practice (Opportunity/Capability):* Cultivate a "needy vulnerability" and practice the sequence in calm, non-triggered states to build psychological muscle memory.
