# Psychic Entropy

**Definition:** A state of internal disorder and distress produced when an individual encounters negative feedback or events that contradict their goals. If severe enough, it compromises the self's ability to concentrate, leading to mental fragmentation.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
This is the fundamental conflict state of consciousness. It drains cognitive resources and forces the self to retreat behind massive defenses if not checked by [[Transformational Coping]].
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Negative environmental feedback, unfulfilled desires, and [[Regressive Coping]] which exacerbates the disorder via a [[Regressive Entropy Spiral]].
- **Downstream (Drives):** Severely decreases available [[Psychic Energy]] and completely inhibits the possibility of [[Flow]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
[[Psychic Entropy]] serves as both an **Opportunity** (the trigger context) and an **Automatic Motivation** driver (the painful drive to restore internal order). 
- **Leverage Point:** Because psychic entropy consumes finite attention, *Feedback-Rich Action Systems* must be designed to interrupt the entropy loop. By providing continuous, clear, and objective external feedback, the environment can pull the user's attention out of their internal turmoil and back into the task.
