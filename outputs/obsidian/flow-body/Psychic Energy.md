# Psychic Energy

**Definition:** The foundational mental resource, fundamentally synonymous with attention, that individuals must invest to cultivate skills, overcome challenges, and process complex experiences.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
[[Psychic Energy]] is the indispensable stock required to drive the [[Complexity Cultivation Loop]]. It is actively invested to impose order on the [[Mind-Body System]] and catalyze [[Flow]]. However, it is highly susceptible to depletion; falling into [[The Hedonic Trap]] by pursuing unearned [[Pleasure]] inhibits and drains [[Psychic Energy]] away from meaningful goals. Without a sufficient stock of [[Psychic Energy]], [[Psychic Entropy]] systematically increases, leading to boredom and anxiety.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Pleasure]] (inhibited by)
- **Downstream (Drives):** [[Flow]], [[Mind-Body System]] (improves), [[Psychic Entropy]] (counteracts)

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
[[Psychic Energy]] functions as a fundamental **Capability** driver. Without it, individuals lack the cognitive bandwidth to structure their experiences.
- **Leverage Point:** Implement foundational attention-training protocols (e.g., sensory isolation or structured breathing exercises like [[Hatha Yoga]]) to increase the individual's available [[Psychic Energy]]. This ensures they possess the cognitive bandwidth required to impose order on the [[Mind-Body System]] rather than being overwhelmed by chaotic stimuli.
