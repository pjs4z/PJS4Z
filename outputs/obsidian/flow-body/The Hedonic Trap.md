# The Hedonic Trap

**Definition:** A reinforcing feedback loop wherein the pursuit of basic biological pleasure without the investment of skill or attention leads to addictive dependence, draining psychic resources and increasing internal cognitive disorder.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
The [[Mind-Body System]] is genetically programmed to seek [[Pleasure]] through basic biological functions requiring no special skills. However, relying purely on [[Pleasure]] without transforming it into a complex activity easily turns into an addictive dependence or meaningless ritual. This dependence inhibits and drains [[Psychic Energy]] away from other meaningful goals. A depleted stock of [[Psychic Energy]] systematically increases [[Psychic Entropy]], which further inhibits the capacity to achieve [[Flow]] or true [[Enjoyment]], trapping the individual in a cycle of seeking basic stimulation to stave off internal chaos.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Pleasure]], [[Mind-Body System]]
- **Downstream (Drives):** [[Psychic Entropy]] (increases), [[Psychic Energy]] (depletes)

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
[[The Hedonic Trap]] acts as an anti-pattern that severely damages **Capability** (by draining attention) and misaligns **Motivation**.
- **Leverage Point:** Deploy wearable technology or digital tools modeled on the [[Experience Sampling Method]] to prompt users at random intervals. This acts as an **Opportunity** trigger that forces individuals to pause, reflect, and evaluate their current ratio of passive consumption to active engagement, helping them break the cycle of [[The Hedonic Trap]].
