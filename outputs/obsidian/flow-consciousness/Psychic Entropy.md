# Psychic Entropy

**Definition:** A state of inner disorder and self-disorganization that occurs when incoming information conflicts with an individual's existing intentions or goals. It diverts attention to undesirable objects, rendering psychic energy unwieldy and ineffective.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
[[Psychic Entropy]] represents the breakdown of the psychological system, initiating a *Vicious Cycle of Psychic Entropy* where distraction breeds further structural weakness.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Triggered by unpredictable external events or environmental stimuli that conflict with the current hierarchy of [[Intention]].
- **Downstream (Drives):** Inhibits [[Attention]] (by forcing psychic energy to dissipate on threats rather than goals) and decreases the structural integrity of [[The Self]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Opportunity/Capability Destroyer:** The presence of [[Psychic Entropy]] strips away the user's functional cognitive bandwidth, making goal-directed behavior impossible.
- **Leverage Point (Cognitive Training):** Implement cognitive reframing protocols that train individuals to categorize unexpected, disruptive information as "neutral" or as a "challenge" rather than a "threat." This halts the dissipation of [[Attention]] and protects [[The Self]] from disorganization.
