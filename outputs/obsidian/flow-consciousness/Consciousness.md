# Consciousness

**Definition:** A self-directed central clearinghouse that represents information about the organism's internal and external environments, allowing sensations, feelings, and ideas to be selectively evaluated and intentionally ordered.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
[[Consciousness]] serves as the foundational arena where subjective reality is constructed. It operates with a strict bandwidth limit, processing only the information selected by [[Attention]].
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Attention]] (which catalyzes it by selecting relevant bits from millions of potential inputs).
- **Downstream (Drives):** Inhibits [[Biological Programming]] (via a balancing loop known as the *Conscious Override of Biological Directives*, allowing the individual to suppress genetic instructions like hunger in favor of higher-order ideological goals).

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability:** Acts as the absolute cognitive bottleneck. Because it can only process ~126 bits of information per second, behavioral interventions must rigorously account for this limit.
- **Leverage Point (Environmental Design):** To optimize [[Consciousness]], physical and digital environments must be engineered to ruthlessly eliminate extraneous information and distractors, thereby preserving bandwidth for goal-directed tasks and avoiding [[Psychic Entropy]].
