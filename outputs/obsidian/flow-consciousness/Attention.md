# Attention

**Definition:** A limited-capacity mental process that selects, retrieves, and evaluates specific bits of information from millions of possibilities to direct conscious experience. Because it is required to accomplish any mental work and dissipates in the process, it functions as the fundamental psychic energy of the mind.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
[[Attention]] is the active energetic currency of the mind, structurally mediating the relationship between [[Intention]] and lived reality. It is the engine of the *Circular Causality of Identity* loop.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Intention]] (which acts as a magnetic field pulling psychic energy) and [[The Self]] (which structurally manages and directs focus).
- **Downstream (Drives):** Catalyzes [[Consciousness]] by populating it with specific data. Increases and shapes [[The Self]] by translating selected information into lived experience. 
- **Inhibited by:** [[Psychic Entropy]] (which scatters and dissipates psychic energy).

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability:** The ~126 bits/second processing limit of [[Attention]] dictates human cognitive capacity. 
- **Motivation:** Highly dependent on hierarchies of [[Intention]] to remain focused.
- **Leverage Point (Opportunity Modification):** Protect this scarce resource by limiting unpredictable external interruptions. Designing environments that naturally channel [[Attention]] toward goal-congruent challenges is the prerequisite for achieving [[Flow]].
