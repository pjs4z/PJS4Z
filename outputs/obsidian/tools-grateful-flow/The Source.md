# The Source

**Definition:** An infinitely generous, creative higher force or presence that generates and sustains all life, providing continuous support and intrinsic energy.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
[[The Source]] is the ultimate positive anchor in [[The Upward Spiral of Gratefulness]]. It continuously streams infinite energy into the psychological system, directly increasing active [[Peace of Mind]] and enduring [[Perspective]]. Access to it is entirely dependent on the catalytic presence of [[Gratefulness]], which is exercised via [[The Grateful Flow]].
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Gratefulness]], [[The Grateful Flow]]
- **Downstream (Drives):** [[Peace of Mind]], [[Perspective]]

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Motivation:** Acts as the ultimate intrinsic motivator. When a user connects to [[The Source]], the reinforcing energy and support felt upon successful connection provides a sustainable alternative to the exhausting, finite drive of [[Adrenaline-based Motivation]].
- **Leverage Point:** Shifting the individual's motivational foundation from "perceived lack" (survival/fear) to "abundant support" prevents the systemic burnout common in chronic worriers.
