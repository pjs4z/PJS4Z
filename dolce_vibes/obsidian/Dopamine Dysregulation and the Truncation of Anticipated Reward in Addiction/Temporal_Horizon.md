# Temporal Horizon

**Type:** Abstract Entity

**Description:** The cognitive boundary and normative temporal window defining how far into the future an organism naturally and spontaneously projects conscious thought and anticipates events. In the context of [[Addiction|Addiction]], this horizon is violently truncated, often reducing from years (e.g., 4.7 years in a [[Healthy_Control_Cohort|Healthy Control Cohort]]) to mere days (e.g., 9 days).
