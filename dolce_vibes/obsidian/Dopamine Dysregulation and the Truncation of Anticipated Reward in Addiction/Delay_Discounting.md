# Delay Discounting

**Type:** Abstract Entity

**Description:** The behavioral economic process and mathematical hyperbolic decay function by which the subjective value of a reward decreases as a direct function of the temporal delay required to receive it. Formally inscribed via [[Mazurs_Hyperbolic_Discounting_Equation|Mazur's Hyperbolic Discounting Equation]].
