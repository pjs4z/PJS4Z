# Immediate Drug Reward (SSR)

**Objective Quality:** High-magnitude pharmacological reinforcement occurring in extreme temporal proximity (within hours or days).
**Subjective Quale:** An overpowering, highly localized surge in craving that completely eclipses the valuation of abstract long-term well-being, precipitating sudden preference reversal. *(See: [[vibe_overpowering_localized_craving|Overpowering Localized Craving]])*
