# Episodic Future Thinking Intervention

## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
* **Pre-State:** The patient operates with a default 9-day [[Temporal_Horizon|temporal horizon]], exhibiting steep [[Delay_Discounting|delay discounting]] and an inability to value distal rewards.
* **Execution:** The patient is instructed to vividly imagine, write about, and generate distinct [[Self_Generated_EFT_Cognitive_Tags|cognitive tags]] for highly positive, personalized events at escalating future delays, which are then presented during a discounting task.
* **Post-State:** Neural coupling between the medial temporal lobe and lateral prefrontal cortex is restored, delay discounting is significantly attenuated, and the patient successfully selects the delayed reward.
