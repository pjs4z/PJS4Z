# Acute Delay Aversion / Psychological Pain of Waiting

## Emotion Frame Elements
* **Experiencer:** [[Individual_with_ADHD|Individual with Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)]]
* **Trigger:** Extended required wait times or unbridged temporal gaps in forced-choice paradigms
* **Appraisal:** The temporal distance is under-stimulated, intolerable, and behaviorally unsustainable.
* **Physiological Substrate:** Failure of midbrain dopamine cells to transfer firing to the reward-predicting cue, leaving the delay unbridged by anticipatory dopamine.
* **Social Display Rule:** Manifests as behavioral disruption, distress, high dropout rates, and seeking immediate environmental stimulation.
