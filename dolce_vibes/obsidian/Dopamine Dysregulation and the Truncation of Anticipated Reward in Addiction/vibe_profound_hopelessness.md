# Profound Hopelessness / The Void of Anticipation

## Emotion Frame Elements
* **Experiencer:** [[Parasuicidal_Individual|Individual with severe clinical depression or parasuicidal ideation]]
* **Trigger:** The cognitive requirement to simulate or look forward to future events
* **Appraisal:** The future is a temporal void entirely devoid of positive, rewarding outcomes.
* **Physiological Substrate:** Complete failure of the anticipatory mechanism itself; inability to generate positive episodic future thought.
* **Social Display Rule:** Expressed through intentional overdose, self-injurious behavior, and the severing of future interpersonal ties.
