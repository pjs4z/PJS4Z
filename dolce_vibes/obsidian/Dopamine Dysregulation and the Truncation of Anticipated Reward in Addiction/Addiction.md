# Addiction

**Type:** Abstract Entity

**Description:** Conceptualized not as a moral failure or acute physical dependence, but as a profound pathology of the [[Temporal_Horizon|temporal horizon]] and a systemic disruption in the perception and valuation of future events. Driven by underlying [[Dopamine_Dysregulation|Dopamine Dysregulation]] and resulting in severe [[Delay_Discounting|Delay Discounting]].
