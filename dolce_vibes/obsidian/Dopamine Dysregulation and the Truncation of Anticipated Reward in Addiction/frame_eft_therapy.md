# Episodic Future Thinking Therapy Frame

**Authorial Perspective:** A mechanistic, cognitive intervention that artificially stretches the truncated [[Temporal_Horizon|temporal horizon]] by restoring neural coupling.

## Frame Elements
* **Agent:** Patient suffering from addiction or severe impulsivity
* **Target:** Abstract Larger-Later Reward (LLR)
* **Trigger:** Explicit instruction to vividly imagine and generate cognitive tags for highly positive future events
* **Prior State:** Default 9-day temporal horizon with steep [[Delay_Discounting|delay discounting]] and inability to value distal rewards
* **Outcome:** Restoration of MTL-lPFC neural coupling, attenuation of delay discounting, and successful selection of delayed rewards *(See: [[Episodic_Future_Thinking_Intervention|EFT Intervention]])*

**Missing Variables:** Spontaneous generation of future goals without explicit clinical prompting.
