# Contingency Management Execution

## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
* **Pre-State:** The patient possesses a severely dysregulated dopamine system incapable of conceptualizing long-term health benefits or engaging in top-down executive exercises.
* **Execution:** The [[Clinical_Therapist|clinician]] demands continuous abstinence for a highly restricted timeframe (2-3 days) and provides immediate, tangible reinforcement (vouchers/prizes) upon objective verification (urinalysis).
* **Post-State:** The immediate voucher outcompetes the proximal drug cue, maintaining short-term abstinence and allowing gradual neuroplastic recovery of D2 receptor function.
