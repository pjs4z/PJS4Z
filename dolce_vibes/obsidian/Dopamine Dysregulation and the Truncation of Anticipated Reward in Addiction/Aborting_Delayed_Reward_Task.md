# Aborting Delayed Reward Task

**Agent:** [[Individual_with_ADHD|Individual with ADHD]]
**Is Caused By Intention:** To abandon the delayed goal and immediately seek alternative environmental sensory stimulation.
**Semantic Justification:** Due to a failure in anticipatory dopamine release, the unbridged temporal gap before a reward is delivered is experienced as psychologically painful and under-stimulated. Aborting the task is a rational, biologically driven escape from the acute subjective distress of waiting.
