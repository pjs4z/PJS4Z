# The Odysseus Protocol (Structural Surrender)

**Authorial Perspective:** Framed as a clinically revolutionary, mathematically sound survival strategy that bypasses the illusion of willpower.

## Frame Elements
* **Agent:** [[The_Subject|The Subject]]
* **Target:** [[The_Subject|The Subject]]'s own neurobiology and ego-driven rationalizations
* **Trigger:** An acute psychological decompensation event and the existential fear of hitting an operational wall in his startup.
* **Prior State:** A reliance on willpower-based survival, narcissistic defense mechanisms, and severe executive dysfunction leading to self-sabotage.
* **Outcome:** [[The_Subject|The Subject]] voluntarily outsources his prefrontal cortex to a trusted peer, mandates absolute transparency, and achieves sustained remission through structural engineering.

**Missing Variables:** The specific identity, background, and internal psychological state of the 'trusted peer proxy' who holds the Subject's executive function.
