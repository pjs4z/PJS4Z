# Bill Wilson's Paradox

**Description:** An unfalsifiable epistemological loop (a Kafka Trap) where the denial of the addiction disease, or the use of logic to deconstruct it, is weaponized as the primary proof of the disease. It is structurally enforced by [[Bill_Wilsons_12_Steps_Literature|Bill Wilson's 12 Steps Literature]].
