# The Subconscious Rebellion

## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
* **Pre-State:** [[The_Subject|The Subject]] is burdened by the transgenerational projection of his prodigy grandfather ([[The_Ghost_Grandfather|The Ghost Grandfather]]), creating an impossible psychological mandate and familial enmeshment.
* **Execution:** [[The_Subject|The Subject]] descends into severe substance abuse (alcohol and prescription stimulants) as a desperate individuation process to down-regulate his processing speed and escape the pressure.
* **Post-State:** [[The_Subject|The Subject]]'s destabilization is met with covert relief (schadenfreude) from his siblings, cementing a deep-seated hyper-vigilance and mistrust of traditional social matrices.
