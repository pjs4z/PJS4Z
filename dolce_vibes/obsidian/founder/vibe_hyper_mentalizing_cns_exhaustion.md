# Hyper-Mentalizing CNS Exhaustion

## Emotion Frame Elements
* **Experiencer:** [[The_Subject|The Subject]]
* **Trigger:** The involuntary, real-time decoding of micro-expressions, hidden agendas, and systemic power dynamics in social settings.
* **Appraisal:** The cognitive evaluation that social matrices are inherently manipulative, cynical, and threatening, requiring constant hyper-vigilance.
