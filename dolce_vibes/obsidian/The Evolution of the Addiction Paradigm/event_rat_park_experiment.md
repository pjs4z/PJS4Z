# Rat Park Experiment

## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
* **Pre-State:** Scientific consensus relies on isolated, caged rats to prove that drugs are inherently and chemically irresistible.
* **Execution:** Researchers place rats in a massive, enriched, mixed-sex social environment and offer them a choice between plain and morphine-laced water.
* **Post-State:** Rats actively avoid the morphine solution, proving that social and environmental integration suppresses drug self-administration.
