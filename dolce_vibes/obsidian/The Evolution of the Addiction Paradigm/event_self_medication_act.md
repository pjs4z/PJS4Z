# Self Medication Act

## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
* **Pre-State:** The individual suffers from profound psychological suffering, unhealed childhood trauma, and severe deficits in affect regulation.
* **Execution:** The individual unconsciously selects and consumes a specific drug whose psychopharmacological action targets their dominant emotional pain.
* **Post-State:** The individual experiences temporary relief from emotional anguish, but develops a destructive, compensatory dependency.
