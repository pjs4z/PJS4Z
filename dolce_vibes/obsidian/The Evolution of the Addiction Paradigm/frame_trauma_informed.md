# Trauma-Informed Self-Medication

**Authorial Perspective:** A psychodynamic and trauma-centric lens that profoundly humanizes the addict by framing destructive behavior as a desperate, adaptive coping mechanism.

## Frame Elements
* **Agent:** [[agent_the_traumatized_addict|The traumatized or psychologically suffering individual]]
* **Target:** A specific psychopharmacological substance (the 'drug of choice')
* **Trigger:** Profound psychological suffering, unhealed childhood trauma, toxic stress, and severe deficits in self-regulation.
* **Prior State:** Chronic emotional overwhelm, agonizing internal disharmony, or a terrifying inability to feel emotions (numbness).
* **Outcome:** Temporary muting or management of specific emotional pain, which ultimately develops into a destructive compensatory dependency.

**Missing Variables:** The moral culpability or hedonic thrill-seeking motives of the user (explicitly rejected by this frame).
