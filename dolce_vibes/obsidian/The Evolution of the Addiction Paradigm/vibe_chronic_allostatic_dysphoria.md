# Chronic Allostatic Dysphoria (The Misery Trap)

## Emotion Frame Elements
* **Experiencer:** [[entity_the_addict|The chronic, late-stage addicted individual.]]
* **Trigger:** The wearing off of the drug and the absence of the substance in the system.
* **Appraisal:** Survival-driven evaluation: 'I am not seeking pleasure; I am desperately trying to escape this agonizing, permanent state of misery and stress.'
* **Physiological Substrate:** Severe overactivity of the extended amygdala (stress center), hyperactivation of the corticotropin-releasing factor (CRF) system, and blunted mesolimbic dopamine.
* **Social Display Rule:** Society and legal systems normatively judge the addict as a hedonistic thrill-seeker, fundamentally misunderstanding and invalidating the desperate, physiological panic driving the behavior.
