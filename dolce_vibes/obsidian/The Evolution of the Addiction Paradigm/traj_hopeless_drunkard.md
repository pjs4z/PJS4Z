# Trajectory: The Hopeless Drunkard

**Process Described:** Restoration to society and achievement of sustained sobriety via mutual aid.

## Conceptual Coordinates
* **Start Coordinates:** Social Integration (0.05), Volitional Agency (0.1)
* **End Coordinates:** Social Integration (0.9), Volitional Agency (0.6)

**Trajectory Catalyst:** Public narrative sharing, the admission of unmanageability, and empathic bonding with peers possessing lived experience, which bypasses moral stigma and alleviates isolation. (See: [[entity_alcoholics_anonymous|Alcoholics Anonymous]])
