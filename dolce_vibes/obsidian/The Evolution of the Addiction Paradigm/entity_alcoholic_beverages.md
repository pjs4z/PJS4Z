# Alcoholic Beverages

**Description:** Chemical substances consumed by individuals, historically viewed variably as healthy tonics, gifts from God, or destructive 'demon rum'.

## Qualities and Qualia (Pre-Industrial Consumption)
* **Objective Quality:** High per capita intake of alcoholic beverages across demographics, including children.
* **Subjective Quale:** Perceived as a healthy tonic, an essential medicine, and a divine gift, with no conscious recognition of chronic disease.
