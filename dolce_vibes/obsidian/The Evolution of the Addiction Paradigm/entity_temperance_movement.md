# Temperance Movement

**Description:** A nineteenth-century social and religious crusade that framed alcohol consumption as a profound threat to national stability and moral character.

## Contextual D&S Pattern: Theology of Intemperance
* **Description Framework:** Theology of Intemperance (Lyman Beecher)
* **Generated Situation:** The habitual drinker is categorized as a moral degenerate and an existential threat to society, requiring total abstinence to save their immortal soul.
* **Perspectival Bias:** A highly moralized, punitive, and religiously driven lens that views addiction purely as a sinful failure of character.
