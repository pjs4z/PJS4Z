# The Disease Concept of Alcoholism (E.M. Jellinek, 1960)

**Causal Force:** Systematically codified the medical framing of alcoholism into distinct typologies, depoliticizing alcohol use, transitioning it to a primary public health concern, and paving the way for AMA recognition and insurance-funded treatment infrastructure.

**Constrains/Empowers:** 
* Academic Researchers
* Clinicians
* Public Health Officials
* Insurance Companies
