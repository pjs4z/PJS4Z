# Trajectory: The Neurobiological Addict

**Process Described:** The progressive cycle of addiction from initial intoxication to chronic preoccupation.

## Conceptual Coordinates
* **Start Coordinates:** Volitional Agency (0.8), Affective Valence (0.95)
* **End Coordinates:** Volitional Agency (0.05), Affective Valence (0.05)

**Trajectory Catalyst:** Repeated substance exposure causing massive dopamine downregulation in the basal ganglia and hyperactivation of the extended amygdala, shifting the brain from homeostatic pleasure-seeking to allostatic misery-escape.
