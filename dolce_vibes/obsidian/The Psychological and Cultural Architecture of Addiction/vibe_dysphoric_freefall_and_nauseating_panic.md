# Dysphoric Freefall and Nauseating Panic
## Emotion Frame Elements
* **Experiencer:** Late-stage addicts (e.g., characters in Requiem for a Dream, [[agent_don_birnam|Don Birnam]] in Bellevue)
* **Trigger:** The failure of neurological reward circuits and the onset of harsh physical withdrawal
* **Appraisal:** The realization of infinite possibilities collapsing into the crushing emptiness of unattainable desires (Kierkegaard's vertigo of freedom)
* **Physiological Substrate:** Severe physical withdrawal, nauseating panic, disorientation, and delirium tremens (the 'screaming horror show')
* **Social Display Rule:** Regression to uterine security (fetal position) and institutional isolation, hidden from normative society
