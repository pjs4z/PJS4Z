# Don Birnam's Weekend Escape
## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
* **Pre-State:** Immobilized by severe writer's block, fear of failure, and scheduled for a rehabilitative country weekend with his brother.
* **Execution:** Deviously engineering a last-minute change to the weekend plan to escape supervision.
* **Post-State:** Gaining access to hidden liquor and local bars, perpetuating a cycle of shame and deception.
