# Codependent Enabling
**Authorial Perspective:** Systemic and tragic; reframes the enabler's actions from romantic loyalty to toxic, harmful codependency that perpetuates the disease.

## Frame Elements
* **Agent:** The [[caretaker_enabler|Caretaker / Enabler]] (e.g., Helen St. James, [[agent_david_sheff|David Sheff]])
* **Target:** The addicted family member
* **Trigger:** The addict's chaotic behavior and the threat of immediate catastrophe
* **Prior State:** A functioning family unit or a state of romantic/parental devotion
* **Outcome:** Severe codependency, resource depletion, deep resentment, and the unintentional perpetuation of the addiction cycle

**Missing Variables:** The enabler's own independent identity or personal needs outside the addict's survival
