# The High-Functioning Illusion Narrative
**Generated Situation:** Protagonists execute elite professional tasks flawlessly (e.g., landing a plane, winning chess tournaments) while their personal lives and internal regulation disintegrate.
**Perspectival Bias:** Paradoxical and mythologizing; glamorizes the 'tortured genius' and dangerously suggests that professional competence or momentary heroism negates the severity of the underlying [[disease_model|disease]].
