# Agonizing Helplessness and Enmeshed Despair
## Emotion Frame Elements
* **Experiencer:** The Codependent Parent/Caretaker (e.g., [[agent_david_sheff|David Sheff]])
* **Trigger:** The continuous, destructive relapses of the addicted child and the failure of repeated rescue attempts
* **Appraisal:** The painful realization that desperate acts of kindness have become harmful enabling, and that parental love cannot cure a brain disorder
* **Physiological Substrate:** Chronic stress, exhaustion, resource depletion, and the volatile oscillation between high-positive attachment and deep-negative despair
* **Social Display Rule:** Maintaining a facade of family stability while internally suffering, eventually requiring the socially difficult act of emotional detachment and boundary setting
