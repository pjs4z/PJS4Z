# High-Functioning Compartmentalization
**Authorial Perspective:** Paradoxical and mythologizing; explores the illusion of control where professional competence masks severe internal dysregulation.

## Frame Elements
* **Agent:** The 'Tortured Genius' or [[high_functioning_addict|High-Functioning Addict]] (e.g., [[agent_beth_harmon|Beth Harmon]], [[agent_whip_whitaker|Whip Whitaker]])
* **Target:** Elite professional or intellectual tasks (e.g., global chess tournaments, flying a commercial airliner)
* **Trigger:** Trauma, stress, and the immense pressure of competition or performance
* **Prior State:** Intact professional capabilities but underlying psychological vulnerability, trauma, or differential depression
* **Outcome:** Catastrophic personal dysregulation, eventual executive collapse, and severe social impairment

**Missing Variables:** Long-term neurobiological sustainability of the compartmentalization
