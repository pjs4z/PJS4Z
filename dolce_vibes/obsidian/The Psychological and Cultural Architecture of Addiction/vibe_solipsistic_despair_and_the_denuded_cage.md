# Solipsistic Despair and the Denuded Cage
## Emotion Frame Elements
* **Experiencer:** Recovering or chronic addicts (e.g., [[agent_don_gately|Don Gately]], characters in Infinite Jest)
* **Trigger:** The lack of environmental enrichment, social connection, and the grueling emotional labor of sustained recovery
* **Appraisal:** Feeling like 'the most disgusting, and hated of creatures,' trapped in one's own mind, recognizing the inescapable need to worship something
* **Physiological Substrate:** Profound emotional isolation, anhedonia, and the endurance of severe physical trauma without synthetic relief
* **Social Display Rule:** Enduring the pain silently or channeling it strictly through the structured, codified worship of support groups like Alcoholics Anonymous
