# Qualities of Late Stage Addiction
**Target Entity:** Late Stage Addiction
* **Objective Quality:** Prefrontal limbic circuit dysfunction, physiological withdrawal, and systemic deprivation.
* **Subjective Quale:** Agonizing dysphoria, nauseating panic, profound solipsism, and the feeling of being trapped in a 'denuded cage'.
