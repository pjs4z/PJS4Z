# The Affected Orbiters
**Type:** Social Entity

**Description:** The surrounding characters whose lives and trajectories are violently warped by the addict, acting as the audience's moral anchors.

## Qualities and Qualia
* **Objective Quality:** Compensatory hyper-competence, forced premature maturation, and an unsustainable surplus of executive function (+0.53).
* **Subjective Quale:** Anticipatory grief, exhaustion, profound quiet trauma, and the unmedicated nightmare of experiencing the addict's chaos while entirely sober.
