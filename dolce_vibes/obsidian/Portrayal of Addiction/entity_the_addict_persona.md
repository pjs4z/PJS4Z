# The Addict Persona
**Type:** Social Entity

**Description:** The central character archetype embodying the addiction, acting as the engine of plot conflict through impulsivity, deceit, and systemic instability.

## Qualities and Qualia
* **Objective Quality:** Systemic behavioral instability, biological brain hijack, and a measurable deficit in executive function (-0.40).
* **Subjective Quale:** Profound shame, existential pain, desperation to get clean, and fleeting moments of pharmacological oblivion or euphoria.
