# Addiction Spiral and Systemic Collapse
## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
* **Pre-State:** The addict experiences a biological brain hijack, existential pain, or a hyper-active mind requiring chemical dulling.
* **Execution:** The addict engages in impulsive substance abuse, deceit, and opportunistic manipulation to acquire drugs or pharmacological relief.
* **Post-State:** The addict's personal life rots, resulting in profound shame and fleeting highs, while the surrounding social ecosystem is violently warped into systemic instability.
