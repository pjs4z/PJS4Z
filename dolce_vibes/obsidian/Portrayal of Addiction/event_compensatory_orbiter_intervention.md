# Compensatory Orbiter Intervention
## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
* **Pre-State:** The addict's executive function drops into a moderate deficit, threatening the family, workplace, or relationship with imminent systemic collapse.
* **Execution:** The orbiter involuntarily assumes a state of hyper-competence, organizing, maturing prematurely, and managing the fallout of the addict's chaos.
* **Post-State:** The orbiter successfully prevents total collapse but is left entirely sober, drowning in anticipatory grief, and carrying profound, quiet trauma.
