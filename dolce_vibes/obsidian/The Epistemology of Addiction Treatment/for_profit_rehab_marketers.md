**Target Action:** [[publishing_inflated_success_rates|Publishing and advertising 75%+ success rates while concealing actual clinical failure rates.]]

* **Belief:** The public and affluent families will pay exorbitant out-of-pocket costs for programs that promise a definitive 'cure' rather than lifelong chronic disease management.
* **Desire:** To maximize facility revenue, maintain high bed occupancy, and attract premium clientele in a $42 billion commercialized ecosystem.
* **Intention:** To selectively curate outcome data by utilizing per-protocol analyses, excluding dropouts, and relying on biased alumni surveys to mathematically guarantee a high success metric.
