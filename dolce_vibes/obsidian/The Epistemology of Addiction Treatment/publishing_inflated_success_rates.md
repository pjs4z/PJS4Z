# Publishing Inflated Success Rates
**Agent:** [[for_profit_rehab_marketers|For-Profit Rehab Marketers]]
**Is Caused By Intention:** To selectively curate outcome data by utilizing per-protocol analyses, excluding dropouts, and relying on biased alumni surveys to mathematically guarantee a high success metric.

**Semantic Justification:** 
By epistemologically framing addiction treatment as an acute, curable condition rather than a chronic illness, marketers rationalize the exclusion of dropouts and relapses as 'non-compliant' outliers. This justifies the presentation of a highly curated, profitable illusion of success to affluent clients seeking a definitive cure.
