# Rejecting Medication-Assisted Treatment
**Agent:** [[traditional_12_step_purist_facilities|Traditional 12-Step Purist Facilities]]
**Is Caused By Intention:** To exclusively utilize behavioral counseling and 12-step facilitation, actively discouraging patients from utilizing evidence-based medications like Naltrexone or Acamprosate.

**Semantic Justification:** 
Grounded in the historical dogma that addiction is a spiritual deficit requiring total abstinence, these facilities rationalize the rejection of FDA-approved neurobiological medications as preserving ideological purity, while conveniently avoiding the overhead costs and regulatory burdens of medical prescribing infrastructure.
