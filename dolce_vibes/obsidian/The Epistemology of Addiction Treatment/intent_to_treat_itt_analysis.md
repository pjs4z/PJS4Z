# Intent-to-Treat (ITT) Analysis
**Type:** Abstract Entity
**Description:** A rigorous epidemiological methodology that includes every admitted patient in the success rate denominator, preventing selection bias and countering the [[survivor_bias_completer_tautology|Survivor Bias Completer Tautology]].
