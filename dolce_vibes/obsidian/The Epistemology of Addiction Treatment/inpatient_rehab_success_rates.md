# Inpatient Rehab Success Rates
**Target Entity:** [[private_addiction_treatment_industry|Private Addiction Treatment Industry]]
**Objective Quality:** Mathematically invalid metrics heavily distorted by loss to follow-up, lack of control groups, and exclusion of dropouts, with actual long-term relapse rates between 40% and 60%.
**Subjective Quale:** Perceived by the public and marketed by facilities as an extraordinary, guaranteed 'cure' exceeding 75% or 80% success, generating [[vibe_affluent_false_hope|Affluent False Hope]].
