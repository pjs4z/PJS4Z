# Twelve-Step Programs
**Type:** Social Entity
**Description:** A spiritual, abstinence-based behavioral framework historically mandated by traditional rehabs, characterized by rigid dogma and rejection of pharmacological interventions like [[medication_assisted_treatment_mat|MAT]].

**Objective Quality:** A non-pharmacological behavioral intervention with an empirical standalone success rate of 5% to 8%.
**Subjective Quale:** Revered by [[traditional_12_step_purist_facilities|Traditional 12-Step Purist Facilities]], families, and society as the undisputed gold standard and spiritual guarantee for lifelong sobriety.
