# Ideological Rejection of Pharmacotherapy
**Authorial Perspective:** Scientifically objective but frustrated; critiques the anachronistic adherence to 12-step dogma over evidence-based neurobiological medicine.

## Frame Elements
* **Agent:** [[traditional_12_step_purist_facilities|Traditional 12-step purist facilities and inpatient rehabs]]
* **Target:** [[medication_assisted_treatment_mat|Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT)]] such as Naltrexone and Acamprosate
* **Trigger:** Ideological rigidity of abstinence-only paradigms and the lack of medical infrastructure to prescribe and monitor pharmacotherapy.
* **Prior State:** Patients suffering from a chronic neurobiological disorder characterized by profound alterations in brain circuitry.
* **Outcome:** Patients are denied highly effective FDA-approved medications, resulting in structurally hindered care, higher relapse rates, and increased healthcare expenditures.

**Missing Variables:**
* Integration of neurobiological medical care
* Evidence-based resource allocation
