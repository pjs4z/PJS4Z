# Stigmatized Attrition and Shame
## Emotion Frame Elements
* **Experiencer:** Patients who relapse or drop out of treatment
* **Trigger:** Returning to heavy substance use and failing to meet the facility's arbitrary criteria for program completion.
* **Appraisal:** Cognitive evaluation of personal failure, guilt, and apathy, often internalizing the belief that they lacked the required 'spiritual willingness'.
* **Physiological Substrate:** Reactivation of the mesolimbic dopamine system, profound physical dependence, and intense drug craving.
* **Social Display Rule:** Cultural norms of the traditional rehab industry dictate that [[patient_relapse|relapse]] is a personal or spiritual failure rather than a medical symptom, prompting the patient to actively avoid contact with providers and disappear into the 'Loss to Follow-Up' cohort.
