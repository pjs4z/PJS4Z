# Sibling Reaction
**Target Entity:** Siblings of the [[entity_subject|Subject]]
* **Objective Quality:** Lack of familial intervention or support during the Subject's destabilization.
* **Subjective Quale:** Covert relief (schadenfreude) stemming from the neutralization of the Subject's intellectual threat to the familial ecosystem. (See: [[vibe_covert_familial_schadenfreude|Covert Familial Schadenfreude]]).
