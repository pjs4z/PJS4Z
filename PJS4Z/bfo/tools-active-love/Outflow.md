# Outflow

**Definition:** An infinite, underlying energetic force and higher emotional state characterized by unrestricted, unconditional giving and acceptance. Operating independently of external circumstances, this enduring force acts as the ultimate antidote to reactive psychological entrapment.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
[[Outflow]] functions as a macro-level balancing force within the human psychological system. It drives the **Outflow Liberation Cycle**: as an individual's internal stock of [[Outflow]] increases, it fundamentally decreases and dissolves the ego-driven [[Fairness Expectation]]. Without the expectation of fairness to fuel it, [[The Maze]] is starved of its energy source, restoring psychological equilibrium.

- **Upstream (Driven by):** Increased by the deliberate practice of [[Active Love]]. 
- **Downstream (Drives):** Decreases [[The Maze]] and decreases [[Fairness Expectation]]. 

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Motivation (Reward Mechanism):** [[Outflow]] provides an immediate, intrinsic energetic reward. When an individual successfully connects to this state, the psychological relief and expansion act as a powerful reinforcer (*Automatic Motivation*) for continuing to use [[Active Love]] in the future. It replaces the exhausting depletion of anger with self-sustaining peace.
