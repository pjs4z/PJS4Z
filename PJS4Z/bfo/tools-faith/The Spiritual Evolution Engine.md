# The Spiritual Evolution Engine

**Definition:** A reinforcing feedback loop where adopting a growth-oriented spiritual framework encourages the practice of emotional tools, which in turn connect the user to transcendent states that validate and deepen the initial framework.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
This reinforcing loop connects the foundational models of the mind to dynamic behavioral action. When an individual adopts the [[Spiritual System]], it provides the psychological framework and permission required to frequently practice [[Active Love]]. Engaging in [[Active Love]] successfully channels and connects the individual to [[Higher Forces]]. The direct, subjective experience of these [[Higher Forces]] acts to catalyze [[Inner Evolution]]. The resulting psychological expansion and relief then loop back to validate and deeply reinforce the individual's foundational belief in the [[Spiritual System]].
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Idiopathic Exhaustion]], [[The Maze]] (as initiators to enter the loop)
- **Downstream (Drives):** Continuous [[Inner Evolution]], sustained psychological resilience.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
This loop operates as the ultimate Motivation flywheel for continuous behavioral execution. 
- **Leverage Point:** Harness periods of external crisis or constraint (where standard environmental coping mechanisms are disabled) to force the user to turn inward. This initial structural constraint pushes them into the loop. Once the first cycle is complete, the intrinsic Motivation generated by experiencing higher affective states sustains the behavior autonomously.
