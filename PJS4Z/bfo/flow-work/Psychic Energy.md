# Psychic Energy

**Definition:** A finite attentional and mental resource invested into tasks, goals, or stimuli, which fosters personal growth when applied to real challenges but is wasted when commanded against one's will or absorbed by passive entertainment.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
[[Psychic Energy]] is the fundamental Stock that fuels the entire conscious system. In the [[Autotelic Growth Spiral]], the willful investment of [[Psychic Energy]] into challenges catalyzes [[Flow]] and builds the [[Autotelic Personality]]. Conversely, in [[The Parasitic Leisure Drain]], it is continuously absorbed and depleted by [[Mass Leisure]], leading directly to [[Apathy]]. The passivity resulting from [[Apathy]] further reduces the individual's capacity to invest [[Psychic Energy]] into active, complex challenges.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Depleted by [[Mass Leisure]], Decreased by [[Apathy]]
- **Downstream (Drives):** Catalyzes [[Flow]]

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
As the core cognitive resource, [[Psychic Energy]] is the foundational **Capability** driver for all deliberate behavioral change and optimal experience.
- **Leverage Point (Environmental Design):** Protect [[Psychic Energy]] by redesigning environments into an [[Autotelic Job]], where clear feedback and goals naturally channel attention rather than fragmenting it.
- **Leverage Point (Choice Architecture):** Shield [[Psychic Energy]] from the parasitic drain of [[Mass Leisure]] by creating physical and digital friction against passive consumption.
