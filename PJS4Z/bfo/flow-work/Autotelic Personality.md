# Autotelic Personality

**Definition:** An individual's developed capacity to create flow experiences even in the most barren environments by independently recognizing opportunities for action, honing skills, and setting reachable goals.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
The [[Autotelic Personality]] functions as a continuous Stock that grows through the reinforcing [[Autotelic Growth Spiral]]. By willfully investing finite [[Psychic Energy]] into everyday tasks, the individual generates occurrences of [[Flow]]. This active investment and subsequent experience of [[Flow]] continuously replenish and expand the capacity of the [[Autotelic Personality]], allowing them to discover even more complex challenges. The presence of this personality type also actively transforms mundane environments into an [[Autotelic Job]].
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Flow]]
- **Downstream (Drives):** [[Flow]], [[Autotelic Job]]

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
This entity acts as a core **Capability** driver for transforming routine tasks into [[Flow]] experiences.
- **Leverage Point (Skill Building):** Train workers in cognitive methods like [[Empathic Identification]] and attention-focusing rituals to help them deliberately discover micro-challenges in their immediate environment, thereby actively fostering an [[Autotelic Personality]].
- **Leverage Point (Cognitive Reframing):** Help individuals reframe unstructured time, protecting them from [[Apathy]] and an over-reliance on [[Mass Leisure]].
