# The Self

**Definition:** The informational construct within consciousness used to represent an individual's identity and psychological boundaries. It dynamically expands and achieves greater complexity after successfully assimilating the challenges mastered during a flow experience.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
[[The Self]] is not a static entity but a dynamic structure built out of the organized investment of attention.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Grown and complexified by [[Enjoyment]], which occurs in the aftermath of a [[Flow]] state.
- **Downstream (Drives):** Catalyzes the deliberate investment of [[Psychic Energy]] into new, increasingly complex pursuits.
- **Feedback Loop:** As [[The Self]] becomes more complex via the [[Evolution of Self-Complexity]], it demands higher challenges to avoid falling back into [[Psychic Entropy]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
[[The Self]] acts as a core Capability driver (representing the existing skill level that must be deployed to match a task's challenges). 
- **Capability Leverage:** Building the reflective capacity and psychological complexity of [[The Self]] is required for individuals to recognize the deceptive nature of external societal goals, allowing them to divest from [[Exotelic Activity]] and choose intrinsic mastery instead.
