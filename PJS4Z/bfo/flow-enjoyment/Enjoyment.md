# Enjoyment

**Definition:** A forward-moving psychological process that occurs when a person goes beyond biological or social expectations to achieve something unexpected. It requires the deliberate investment of attention and results in psychological growth and increased complexity of the self.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
[[Enjoyment]] is the structural outcome of mastering a challenge, fundamentally different from homeostatic [[Pleasure]], which merely restores baseline equilibrium. 
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Directly generated by sustaining [[Flow]] during an [[Autotelic Activity]].
- **Downstream (Drives):** Increases the complexity, structural order, and capabilities of [[The Self]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
[[Enjoyment]] functions as the ultimate intrinsic, reflective Motivation driver for sustained engagement. 
- **Motivation Leverage:** To break the "Alienation Trap" driven by the pursuit of [[Exotelic Activity]], corporate and educational reward frameworks must shift away from culturally conditioned [[Symbols of Happiness]] (which only yield fleeting [[Pleasure]]) and instead be redesigned to yield genuine [[Enjoyment]] through mastery and novelty.
