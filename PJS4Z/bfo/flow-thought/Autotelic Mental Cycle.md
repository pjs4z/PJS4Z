# Autotelic Mental Cycle

**Definition:** A reinforcing feedback loop wherein intrinsic motivation drives the voluntary investment of psychic energy into the rules of a symbolic system, generating mental flow, which in turn yields profound enjoyment that further deepens intrinsic motivation.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
This reinforcing loop is the engine of lifelong learning and the pursuit of "amateur" sciences and arts. As the individual cycles through this loop, the complexity of their internal world grows, making it easier to achieve subsequent states of flow.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Intrinsic Motivation]], [[Psychic Energy]], [[Symbolic System]]
- **Downstream (Drives):** Increased threshold for [[Mental Flow]], expansion of [[Memory]]

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
This loop thrives when **Motivation** is strictly autotelic (done for its own sake). Behavioral interventions should focus on reframing activities away from professionalized, extrinsic goals (like monetization or career advancement) and toward the concept of the "passionate amateur." By valuing the subjective enjoyment of playing with ideas, the loop sustains itself without needing external reinforcement or rewards.
