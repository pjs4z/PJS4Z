# Psychic Energy

**Definition:** The finite attentional capacity and mental resource that individuals invest into consciously chosen, realistic tasks or goals in order to bring structural order to [[Consciousness]].

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
[[Psychic Energy]] acts as the fundamental currency of the mind. Its allocation determines whether an individual falls prey to external chaos or achieves mastery over their inner life.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Directed by individual agency and cognitive self-regulation, often in opposition to the automatic reflexes of [[Genetic Programming]] and the conditioned pulls of [[Socialization]].
- **Downstream (Drives):** The targeted investment of this energy catalyzes [[Optimal Experience]]. 

Within **[[The Cycle of Emancipation]]**, the successful deployment of [[Psychic Energy]] brings order to [[Consciousness]], which in turn frees up *more* mental bandwidth to be invested into increasingly complex challenges.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
[[Psychic Energy]] is the core **Capability** required for cognitive self-regulation. 
- **Leverage Point:** To enhance an individual's psychological capability to achieve [[Optimal Experience]], interventions (such as mindfulness or attentional training protocols) must focus on expanding the individual's capacity to marshal and protect their [[Psychic Energy]] from the highly distracting, externally manufactured goals of the [[Escalation of Expectations]].
