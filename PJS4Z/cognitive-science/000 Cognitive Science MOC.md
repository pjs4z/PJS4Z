# 000 Cognitive Science MOC

**Cognitive Science** is the interdisciplinary, scientific study of the mind and its processes. It maps an expansive semantic space concerned with the nature of intelligence, representation, behavior, computation, and conscious experience—whether in biological organisms, artificial agents, or distributed systems.

## Core Meta-Questions
- **Representation:** How is information encoded, processed, and retrieved?
- **Embodiment:** To what extent is cognition bound to physical substrates (e.g., the brain, the body, the environment)?
- **Consciousness:** Can subjective conscious experience (qualia) be reduced to computational or dynamical processes?
