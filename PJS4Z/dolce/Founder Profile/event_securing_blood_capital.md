# Securing Blood Capital
## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
* **Pre-State:** The [[entity_subject|Subject]] recognizes the systemic corruption of traditional [[entity_venture_capital|Venture Capital]], which demands chronicity and high LTV/CAC metrics.
* **Execution:** The Subject rejects traditional VC and secures funding exclusively from elite families motivated purely by finding a cure for their own addicted children ([[entity_blood_capital|Blood Capital]]).
* **Post-State:** The Subject structurally insulates his behavioral health mission from market corruption, enabling the architecture of a new ontology of metacognition.

**Agent:** [[entity_subject|Subject]]

* **Desire:** To structurally insulate the behavioral health platform from market corruption and architect a flawless system.
* **Intention:** To fund the platform exclusively through families motivated by existential desperation rather than financial return.
