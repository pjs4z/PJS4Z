# Covert Familial Schadenfreude
## Emotion Frame Elements
* **Experiencer:** Siblings of the [[entity_subject|Subject]]
* **Trigger:** The Subject's destabilization and descent into severe substance abuse
* **Appraisal:** Evaluation that the Subject's intellectual superiority is neutralized, thereby removing a threat to their own standing in the familial ecosystem
* **Physiological Substrate:** Covert tension release and down-regulation of intra-familial threat response
* **Social Display Rule:** Must remain covert and unexpressed publicly; maintaining the outward appearance of familial concern while withholding actual intervention
