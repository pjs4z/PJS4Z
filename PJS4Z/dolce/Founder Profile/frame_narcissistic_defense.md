# Narcissistic Defense (Violence of Action)
**Authorial Perspective:** Objective clinical observation of a historically maladaptive, hyper-intellectualized defense mechanism.

## Frame Elements
* **Agent:** [[entity_subject|Subject]]
* **Target:** Perceived social aggressor
* **Trigger:** Perception of threat or condescension within the social matrix
* **Prior State:** Hyper-vigilance and central nervous system exhaustion from involuntary hyper-mentalizing
* **Outcome:** Public exposure of the aggressor's deepest psychological vulnerability, resulting in absolute social invulnerability but profound isolation
