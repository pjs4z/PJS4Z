# Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT)
**Type:** Physical Entity
**Description:** FDA-approved pharmacological interventions (e.g., Naltrexone, Acamprosate) that normalize brain chemistry and block euphoric effects.

**Conceptual Coordinates:**
* Epistemic Rigor: 0.9
* Treatment Paradigm: 1.0
* Commercial Exploitation: 0.2
* Patient Vulnerability: 0.2
* *Justification:* MAT represents the pinnacle of the neurobiological treatment paradigm, supported by rigorous, peer-reviewed meta-analyses and [[fda_approvals_for_mat_pharmacotherapy|FDA Approvals]]. It significantly reduces patient vulnerability to [[patient_relapse|Patient Relapse]] and lowers overall healthcare expenditures.
