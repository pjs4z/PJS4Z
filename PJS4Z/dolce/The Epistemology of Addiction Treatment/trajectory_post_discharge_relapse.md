# Trajectory: Post-Discharge Relapse
**Entity Tracked:** Treated Patient Cohort (Post-Discharge)
**Process Described:** The temporal degradation of abstinence and survival rate following acute inpatient treatment.

## Conceptual Coordinates
* **Start Coordinates:** Patient Vulnerability (0.3), Treatment Paradigm (0.2)
* **End Coordinates:** Patient Vulnerability (0.8), Treatment Paradigm (0.2)

**Trajectory Catalyst:** Cessation of the protective acute inpatient environment and exposure to profound neurobiological cravings without the integration of long-term pharmacological support ([[medication_assisted_treatment_mat|MAT]]).
