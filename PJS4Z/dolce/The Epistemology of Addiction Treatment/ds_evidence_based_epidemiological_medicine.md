# D&S: Evidence-Based Epidemiological Medicine
**Description Framework:** Evidence-Based Epidemiological Medicine ([[intent_to_treat_itt_analysis|ITT]] & [[medication_assisted_treatment_mat|MAT]]).
**Generated Situation:** Independent researchers use Intent-to-Treat analysis, survival curves, and control groups to demonstrate that Medication-Assisted Treatment and IOPs are highly effective, while traditional 28-day inpatient cures are a myth.
**Perspectival Bias:** Scientific realism and epistemic integrity, prioritizing verifiable long-term clinical outcomes and health economics over historical tradition and profit.
