# The Florida Shuffle Patient Brokering Scheme
**Authorial Perspective:** Condemnatory; frames the practice as a lethal, predatory economic exploitation of vulnerable individuals under the guise of healthcare.

## Frame Elements
* **Agent:** [[florida_shuffle_rogue_operators|Rogue operators and unscrupulous rehab facilities]]
* **Target:** Vulnerable out-of-state patients with severe substance use disorders and robust insurance policies.
* **Trigger:** [[the_affordable_care_act_essential_health_benefits_mandate|The Affordable Care Act's removal of lifetime caps]] on addiction treatment, which created a limitless reimbursement stream.
* **Prior State:** Patients seeking help for severe addiction, possessing active health insurance but lacking local social capital.
* **Outcome:** Patients are continuously cycled through detox and IOP loops, insurance benefits are exhausted, and overdose mortality surges.

**Missing Variables:**
* Clinical stabilization
* Safe discharge to a higher level of care upon relapse
* Genuine therapeutic intent
