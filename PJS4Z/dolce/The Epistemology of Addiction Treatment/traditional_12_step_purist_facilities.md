# Traditional 12-Step Purist Facilities
**Target Action:** [[rejecting_medication_assisted_treatment|Rejecting Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT) and mandating abstinence-only behavioral models.]]

## BDI Triad
* **Belief:** [[addiction_aud_sud|Addiction]] is fundamentally a spiritual deficit or lack of willingness, and the use of FDA-approved pharmacological interventions violates the rigid dogma of total abstinence.
* **Desire:** To preserve historical institutional traditions, ideological purity, and a business model that does not require expensive medical infrastructure or prescribing physicians.
* **Intention:** To exclusively utilize behavioral counseling and [[twelve_step_programs|12-step facilitation]], actively discouraging patients from utilizing evidence-based medications like Naltrexone or Acamprosate.

## Social Moments
* Imposing subjective, abstract spiritual dogma as a substitute for neurobiological medical treatment.
* Creating structural barriers to effective care, thereby increasing the patient's statistical probability of [[patient_relapse|relapse]].
