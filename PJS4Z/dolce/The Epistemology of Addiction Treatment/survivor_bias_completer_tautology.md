# Survivor Bias Completer Tautology
**Type:** Abstract Entity
**Description:** A statistical manipulation technique where treatment centers only count patients who successfully complete the program, discarding dropouts to inflate [[inpatient_rehab_success_rates|Inpatient Rehab Success Rates]].
