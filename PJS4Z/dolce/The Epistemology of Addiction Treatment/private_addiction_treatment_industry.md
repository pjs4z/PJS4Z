# Private Addiction Treatment Industry
**Type:** Social Entity
**Description:** A highly commercialized, for-profit ecosystem valued at $35-$42 billion annually that markets acute-care addiction treatments.

**Conceptual Coordinates:**
* Epistemic Rigor: 0.1
* Treatment Paradigm: 0.1
* Commercial Exploitation: 0.9
* Patient Vulnerability: 0.4
* *Justification:* The industry operates with extremely low epistemic rigor, utilizing [[statistical_manipulation_of_outcomes|Statistical Manipulation of Outcomes]] and [[survivor_bias_completer_tautology|Completer Bias]] to project 75% success rates.
