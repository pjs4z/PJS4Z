# The Florida Shuffle Patient Brokering
## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
* **Pre-State:** Vulnerable out-of-state individuals with severe [[addiction_aud_sud|substance use disorders]] seek treatment, possessing insurance policies with no lifetime caps due to [[the_affordable_care_act_essential_health_benefits_mandate|The Affordable Care Act]].
* **Execution:** [[florida_shuffle_rogue_operators|Rogue operators]] recruit these patients using deceptive marketing or illegal kickbacks, cycle them repeatedly through substandard detox and intensive outpatient programs, and subject them to excessive, highly billed daily urinalysis.
* **Post-State:** Patients' insurance benefits are completely exhausted, clinical stabilization fails, and patients are either brokered to new facilities upon [[patient_relapse|relapse]] or suffer fatal overdoses at rates vastly exceeding the local population.
