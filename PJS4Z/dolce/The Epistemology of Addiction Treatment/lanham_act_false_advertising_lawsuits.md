# Lanham Act False Advertising Lawsuits
**Causal Force:** Imposes legal and financial penalties on facilities that weaponize language (e.g., claiming a 'cure') and misrepresent clinical realities, forcing corporate accountability and restricting deceptive marketing.
**Constrains/Empowers:** Luxury Rehabilitation Centers, Legal Authorities, Deceived Consumers.
