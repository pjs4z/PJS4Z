# Statistical Manipulation of Outcomes
## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
* **Pre-State:** [[private_addiction_treatment_industry|For-profit rehabilitation centers]] collect raw clinical data from admitted patients, which includes a high volume of dropouts, severe comorbidities, and early relapses.
* **Execution:** Facilities systematically apply 'completer bias' by discarding data from patients who leave against medical advice, utilizing abbreviated follow-up periods, ignoring loss to follow-up (LTFU), and lacking control groups.
* **Post-State:** The facility publishes artificially inflated [[inpatient_rehab_success_rates|success rates]] (often exceeding 75%) in commercial marketing materials, obscuring the true 40% to 60% clinical relapse rate.
