**Target Action:** [[cycling_patients_in_florida_shuffle|Cycling patients through continuous detox/IOP loops and billing for fraudulent or excessive diagnostics.]]

* **Belief:** [[the_affordable_care_act_essential_health_benefits_mandate|The Affordable Care Act's essential health benefits mandate]] provides a limitless, highly lucrative reimbursement stream from insurance providers for addiction services and urinalysis.
* **Desire:** To extract maximum financial profit from insurance companies with minimal expenditure on actual clinical care.
