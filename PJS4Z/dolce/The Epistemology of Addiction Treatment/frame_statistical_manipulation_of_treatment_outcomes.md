# Statistical Manipulation of Treatment Outcomes
**Authorial Perspective:** Highly critical and epidemiological; views the industry's success metrics as deceptive fabrications designed to obscure clinical reality and prioritize profit.

## Frame Elements
* **Agent:** [[for_profit_rehab_marketers|For-profit rehabilitation centers and industry marketers]]
* **Target:** Clinical outcome data and success rate statistics
* **Trigger:** The commercial imperative to market high success rates (e.g., 75%+) to attract paying clients in a $42 billion ecosystem.
* **Prior State:** Raw clinical data reflecting the reality of addiction as a chronic, relapsing condition with high dropout and attrition rates.
* **Outcome:** The publication of artificially inflated success rates that mislead the public by exploiting [[survivor_bias_completer_tautology|completer tautologies]].

**Missing Variables:**
* Data from treatment dropouts (the uncounted 25%+)
* Data from patients lost to follow-up (LTFU)
* Untreated control groups for baseline comparison
