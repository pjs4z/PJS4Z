# Intent-to-Treat (ITT) Analysis Protocol
**Causal Force:** A rigorous epidemiological standard that, if mandated by federal regulatory bodies, would force facilities to include all admitted patients in outcome metrics, destroying the illusion of 75% success rates and constraining marketing fabrication.
**Constrains/Empowers:** Federal Regulatory Bodies, Independent Researchers, [[for_profit_rehab_marketers|For-Profit Rehab Marketers]].
