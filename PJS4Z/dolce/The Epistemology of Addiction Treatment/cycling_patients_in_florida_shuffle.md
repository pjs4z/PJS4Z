# Cycling Patients in Florida Shuffle
**Agent:** [[florida_shuffle_rogue_operators|Florida Shuffle Rogue Operators]]
**Is Caused By Intention:** To recruit out-of-state patients, incentivize their relapse, and trade them as commodities ('heads in beds') to trigger new cycles of lucrative insurance billing.

**Semantic Justification:** 
Operating under a fee-for-service model where maximum revenue is generated during acute phases, operators rationalize [[patient_relapse|patient relapse]] not as a clinical failure, but as a necessary, highly profitable administrative event within the limitless reimbursement ecosystem created by healthcare legislation.
