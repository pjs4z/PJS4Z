# AA Matrix

**Description:** The 90-year-old institutional, cultural, and dogmatic framework of Alcoholics Anonymous and the traditional rehab establishment. It enforces [[The_Addict_Label|The Addict Label]] and relies heavily on [[Bill_Wilsons_Paradox|Bill Wilson's Paradox]].

## Qualities & Qualia
* **AA Meeting Environment:**
  * *Objective Quality:* A gathering of individuals sharing addiction narratives with a statistical 3% long-term success rate.
  * *Subjective Quale:* A social competition for status based on vulgarity; a psychological torture chamber for a hyper-analytical mind forced to submit to a broken algorithm.
