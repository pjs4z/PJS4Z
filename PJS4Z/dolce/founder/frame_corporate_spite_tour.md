# The Corporate Spite Tour (Weaponized Competence)

**Authorial Perspective:** Viewed as a subconscious detonation and a desperate individuation process driven by an under-stimulated ADHD brain, rather than mere professional ambition.

## Frame Elements
* **Agent:** [[The_Subject|The Subject]] (possessing a 150+ IQ and severe executive dysfunction)
* **Target:** The corporate elite, institutional authority figures, and peers exhibiting [[Tall_Poppy_Syndrome|Tall Poppy Syndrome]]
* **Trigger:** The condescension, fake empathy, and covert schadenfreude of peers, combined with a severe dopamine famine from under-stimulation.
* **Prior State:** A state of chronic interpersonal cynicism, familial enmeshment, and being viewed as a self-destructive outcast.
* **Outcome:** [[The_Subject|The Subject]] effortlessly conquers elite corporate environments (McKinsey, SoftBank), wins 'Employee of the Year', and then violently burns the bridges to cleanse himself of a morally bankrupt system.

**Missing Variables:** The objective emotional or professional fallout experienced by the targeted corporate entities (their internal state post-burn is omitted).
