# Tragic Spiraling Shame
## Emotion Frame Elements
* **Experiencer:** [[agent_the_tragic_victim|The Tragic Victim]] / Spiraling Sufferer
* **Trigger:** Repeatedly failing to get clean and causing immense collateral harm to loved ones.
* **Appraisal:** The agonizing realization that their actions are destructive, coupled with the belief that their brain is biologically hijacked, rendering them powerless to stop the cycle.
* **Physiological Substrate:** Profound suffering, agony, biological cravings, and the visceral pain of withdrawal or intoxication.
* **Social Display Rule:** Expressed as a symptom of a biopsychosocial illness rather than inherent malice, culturally designed to elicit pity and tragic sympathy from the audience rather than moral condemnation.
