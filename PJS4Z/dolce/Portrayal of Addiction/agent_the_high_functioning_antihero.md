# The High Functioning Antihero
**Target Action:** Maintaining professional dominance while allowing personal life to rot through substance abuse.

## Qualities and Qualia
* **Objective Quality:** Rigorous professional compartmentalization and high domain-specific competence alongside severe personal life deterioration.
* **Subjective Quale:** The internal sensation of a hyper-active mind and the existential burden of brilliance that requires chemical dulling.

## BDI Triad
* **Belief:** Believes their brilliance causes existential pain and a hyper-active mind that must be artificially managed.
* **Desire:** Craves the dulling of existential pain and the quieting of their hyper-active mind.
* **Intention:** Commits to compartmentalizing the addiction, using substances to function at a hyper-competent level professionally while abandoning personal obligations.

## Intentional Relations (Dennett's Intentional Stance)
* **Is Caused By Intention:** Commits to compartmentalizing the addiction, using substances to function at a hyper-competent level professionally while abandoning personal obligations.
* **Semantic Justification:** The agent justifies their substance use as a necessary cognitive tool to quiet a hyper-active mind and dull the existential pain of brilliance, viewing personal decay as an acceptable trade-off for professional mastery.

## Social Moments
* Romanticization of their tortured genius by society
* Failure to fulfill interpersonal obligations and emotional reciprocity
