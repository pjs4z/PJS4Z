# Washingtonian Narrative Sharing

## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
* **Pre-State:** Drunkards are viewed as hopeless, morally lost, and isolated within a highly moralized, punitive society.
* **Execution:** Working-class alcoholics publicly recount their descent into alcoholism and recovery to build identification and hope among peers.
* **Post-State:** Participants are temporarily restored to society through peer support, though the movement eventually collapses due to lack of organization and religious backlash.

**Primary Agents:** [[agent_the_washingtonians|The Washingtonians]]
