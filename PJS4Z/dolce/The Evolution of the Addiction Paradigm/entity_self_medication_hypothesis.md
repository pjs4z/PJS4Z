# Self Medication Hypothesis

**Description:** A psychodynamic theory arguing that substance use originates as a compensatory attempt to assuage profound psychological suffering and self-regulation deficits.

## Contextual D&S Pattern
* **Description Framework:** Self-Medication Hypothesis (Edward Khantzian)
* **Generated Situation:** The addict unconsciously selects a specific drug (e.g., heroin for rage, alcohol for isolation) to psychopharmacologically mute their specific, dominant emotional pain.
* **Perspectival Bias:** A psychodynamic, trauma-centric lens that profoundly humanizes the addict by framing their destructive behavior as a desperate, adaptive coping mechanism for psychological suffering.
