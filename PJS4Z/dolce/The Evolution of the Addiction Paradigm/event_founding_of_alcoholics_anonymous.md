# Founding of Alcoholics Anonymous

## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
* **Pre-State:** Institutional treatments are failing, Prohibition has collapsed, and chronic alcoholics are viewed by the medical establishment as untreatable.
* **Execution:** [[agent_bill_wilson_and_dr_bob|Bill Wilson and Dr. Bob]] share their unvarnished struggles, synthesize medical etiologies with spiritual remedies, and draft the Big Book with inclusive language.
* **Post-State:** A globally influential mutual aid society is established, fundamentally shifting the cultural vocabulary of addiction to 'powerlessness' and 'unmanageability'.
