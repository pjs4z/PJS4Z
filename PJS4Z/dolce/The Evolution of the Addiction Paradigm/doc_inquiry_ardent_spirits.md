# An Inquiry into the Effects of Ardent Spirits upon the Human Body and Mind (Benjamin Rush, 1784)

**Causal Force:** Fundamentally altered Western discourse by categorizing habitual drunkenness as an 'odious disease' rather than a mere sin, laying the intellectual groundwork for the medical model and establishing the concept that a chemical substance can physically hijack [[entity_the_human_will|The Human Will]].

**Constrains/Empowers:** 
* Medical Professionals
* The Public
* Compulsive Drinkers
