# The Relapse Process (AVE)

## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
* **Pre-State:** An individual attempting abstinence faces high-risk situations, poor coping skills, and accumulated daily stress.
* **Execution:** The individual experiences a minor lapse (e.g., a single drink), triggering intense guilt, self-blame, and a perceived absolute loss of control.
* **Post-State:** The cognitive reaction (Abstinence Violation Effect) transforms the brief, correctable slip into a full, catastrophic relapse.
