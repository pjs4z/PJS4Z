# National Prohibition (US Law, 1920-1933)

**Causal Force:** Imposed a strict legal ban on alcohol that catastrophically failed, fueling organized crime, closing institutional treatment centers, and creating a massive vacuum in addiction care that necessitated the rise of mutual aid societies like [[entity_alcoholics_anonymous|AA]].

**Constrains/Empowers:** 
* The US Government
* The Public
* Alcoholics
* Organized Crime
