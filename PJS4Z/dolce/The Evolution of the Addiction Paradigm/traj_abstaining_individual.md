# Trajectory: The Abstaining Individual

**Process Described:** The cognitive-behavioral transition from a minor lapse to a full catastrophic relapse.

## Conceptual Coordinates
* **Start Coordinates:** Volitional Agency (0.7), Affective Valence (0.6)
* **End Coordinates:** Volitional Agency (0.05), Affective Valence (0.1)

**Trajectory Catalyst:** The Abstinence Violation Effect (AVE): the cognitive appraisal of a single slip through a rigid, black-and-white moral lens, triggering intense guilt, self-blame, and a perceived absolute loss of control. (See: [[vibe_ave_guilt_spiral|AVE Guilt Spiral]])
