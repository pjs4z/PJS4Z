# Six Sermons on Intemperance (Lyman Beecher, 1827)

**Causal Force:** Established the theological and moral groundwork for 'teetotalism', heavily stigmatizing the addict as a moral degenerate and an existential threat to the republic, which drove punitive societal attitudes and policies.

**Constrains/Empowers:** 
* [[entity_temperance_movement|Temperance Movement]] Advocates
* The Public
* Habitual Drinkers
