# The Abstinence Violation Effect (AVE) Guilt Spiral

## Emotion Frame Elements
* **Experiencer:** [[agent_the_abstaining_addict|The abstaining addict who experiences a minor lapse.]]
* **Trigger:** The physical consumption of a single drink or drug after a period of abstinence.
* **Appraisal:** Black-and-white cognitive evaluation: 'I have absolutely lost control; I am a catastrophic moral failure; my sobriety is completely ruined.'
* **Physiological Substrate:** Heightened systemic stress response, acute anxiety, and the cognitive dissonance associated with breaking a rigid commitment.
* **Social Display Rule:** Traditional abstinence models and societal stigma dictate that any use is a complete moral and medical failure, enforcing profound self-blame and driving the individual to hide the behavior.
