# Alcoholics Anonymous

**Description:** A peer-led mutual aid fellowship that synthesized medical etiologies with practical spiritual remedies to treat alcoholism. Founded by [[agent_bill_wilson_and_dr_bob|Bill Wilson and Dr. Bob]].

## Contextual D&S Pattern: AA Hybrid Disease Model
* **Description Framework:** AA Hybrid Disease Model (Silkworth & Wilson)
* **Generated Situation:** The alcoholic admits complete powerlessness over a physical allergy to alcohol and the unmanageability of their life, seeking recovery through peer support and a spiritual awakening.
* **Perspectival Bias:** A pragmatic, lived-experience perspective that bypasses dogmatic theology to alleviate moral shame while demanding rigorous personal inventory and absolute abstinence.
