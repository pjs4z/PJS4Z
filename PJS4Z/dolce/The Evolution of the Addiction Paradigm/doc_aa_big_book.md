# Alcoholics Anonymous: The Big Book (Bill Wilson, 1939)

**Causal Force:** Shifted the cultural vocabulary of addiction to 'powerlessness' and 'unmanageability', bypassing dogmatic religion to establish the 12-step mutual aid framework as a globally dominant, accessible treatment model.

**Constrains/Empowers:** 
* Recovering Alcoholics
* [[entity_alcoholics_anonymous|AA]] Members
* Addiction Counselors
