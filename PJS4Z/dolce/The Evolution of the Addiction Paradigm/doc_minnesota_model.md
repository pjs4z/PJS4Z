# The Minnesota Model (Clinical Framework)

**Causal Force:** Revolutionized inpatient treatment by integrating professional clinicians with recovering addicts, mandating [[entity_alcoholics_anonymous|AA]] participation, and utilizing the disease model to alleviate moral stigma, setting the ubiquitous global standard for the commercial rehab industry.

**Constrains/Empowers:** 
* Inpatient Rehab Facilities
* Addiction Counselors
* Patients
* Families
