# Dislocation Theory

**Description:** A sociological framework positing that addiction is an adaptive response to the profound social and cultural dislocation caused by modern globalization. Championed by [[agent_bruce_alexander|Bruce Alexander]].

## Contextual D&S Pattern
* **Description Framework:** Dislocation Theory (Bruce Alexander)
* **Generated Situation:** Individuals suffering from a 'poverty of the spirit' due to the dismantling of traditional communities by free-market capitalism use substances to artificially simulate connection and purpose.
* **Perspectival Bias:** A macro-sociological, anti-reductionist lens that views addiction as a structural political failure requiring societal reintegration rather than individual medical treatment.
