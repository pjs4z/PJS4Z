# Rat Park

**Description:** An enriched, highly social experimental housing colony for rats, used to demonstrate that environmental context overrides pharmacological determinism. Conducted by [[agent_bruce_alexander|Bruce Alexander]].

## Conceptual Coordinates: The Enriched Subject
* **Plot:** Volitional Agency (0.85), Etiological Substrate (0.95), Affective Valence (0.9), Social Integration (1.0).
* **Justification:** Placed in a mixed-sex community with play areas, the subject actively chooses to avoid morphine, proving that a highly enriched, connected environment overrides pharmacological determinism.
