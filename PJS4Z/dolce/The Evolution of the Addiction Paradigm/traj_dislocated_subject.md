# Trajectory: The Dislocated Subject

**Process Described:** The suppression of compulsive drug self-administration through environmental enrichment.

## Conceptual Coordinates
* **Start Coordinates:** Social Integration (0.0), Affective Valence (0.1)
* **End Coordinates:** Social Integration (0.95), Affective Valence (0.85)

**Trajectory Catalyst:** Removal from an austere, isolated laboratory cage (or a dislocating capitalist society) and integration into a highly social, enriched community that provides alternative non-drug rewards and psychosocial meaning. (See: [[entity_rat_park|Rat Park]])
