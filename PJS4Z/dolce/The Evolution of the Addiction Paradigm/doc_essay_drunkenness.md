# Essay, Medical, Philosophical and Chemical on Drunkenness (Thomas Trotter, 1804)

**Causal Force:** Moved the locus of addiction pathology away from purely somatic symptoms into the realm of psychiatry by categorizing it as a 'disease of the mind', foreshadowing modern psychiatric classifications.

**Constrains/Empowers:** 
* Psychiatrists
* Medical Professionals
* [[entity_the_addict|Addicts]]
