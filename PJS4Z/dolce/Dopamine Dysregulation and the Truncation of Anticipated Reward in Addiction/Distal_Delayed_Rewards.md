# Distal Delayed Rewards

**Objective Quality:** Chronological distance of months or years (e.g., 4.7 years) from the present moment.
**Subjective Quale:** A subjective utility of absolute zero; computationally invisible and conceptually inaccessible to the addicted brain during decision-making.
