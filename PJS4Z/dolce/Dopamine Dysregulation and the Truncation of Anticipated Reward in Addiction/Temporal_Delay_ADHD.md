# Temporal Delay in ADHD

**Objective Quality:** Extended required wait times and unbridged temporal gaps in forced-choice paradigms.
**Subjective Quale:** Acute, biologically driven psychological pain, distress, and severe aversion to the subjective act of waiting itself. *(See: [[vibe_acute_delay_aversion|Acute Delay Aversion]])*
