# Explicit-Zero and Date Cognitive Framing

**Generated Situation:** Reformulating a choice from '$5 today and $8 later' to '$5 today and $0 later, OR $0 today and $8 later', or using specific calendar dates instead of generic delays.
**Perspectival Bias:** Demonstrating that the [[Temporal_Horizon|temporal horizon]] is a highly malleable semantic construct; altering the linguistic framing of a choice forces cognitive attention forward and alters decision-making without requiring additional willpower.
