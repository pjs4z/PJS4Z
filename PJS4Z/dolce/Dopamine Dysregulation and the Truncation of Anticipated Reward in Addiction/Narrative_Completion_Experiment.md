# Narrative Completion Experiment

## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
* **Pre-State:** Researchers require a methodology to measure spontaneous future-oriented thinking without triggering explicit social desirability bias or hypothetical monetary valuation.
* **Execution:** Researchers present heroin-addicted individuals and healthy controls with a [[Standardized_Narrative_Story_Stems|Standardized Narrative Story Stem]] ('After awakening, Bill...') and instruct them to complete the narrative.
* **Post-State:** Researchers extract the temporal setting of the generated events, revealing a stark dichotomy: healthy controls project 4.7 years into the future, while addicted subjects project only 9 days.
