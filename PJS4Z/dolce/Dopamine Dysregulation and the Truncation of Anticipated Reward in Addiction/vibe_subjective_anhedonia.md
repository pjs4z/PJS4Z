# Subjective Anhedonia towards Natural Rewards

## Emotion Frame Elements
* **Experiencer:** [[Addicted_Individual|Individual with chronic substance use disorder]]
* **Trigger:** Exposure to standard, non-drug rewards (hobbies, social interactions, career advancements)
* **Appraisal:** These previously anticipated events hold no subjective value or interest.
* **Physiological Substrate:** Massive downregulation of dopamine D2 receptors in the striatum and profoundly blunted Reward Prediction Error (RPE) signals.
* **Social Display Rule:** Withdrawal from normative social life, loss of interest in long-term goals, and flat affect toward everyday positive stimuli.
