# Consuming Immediate Drug (Relapse)

**Agent:** [[Addicted_Individual|Addicted Individual]]
**Is Caused By Intention:** To acquire and consume the drug immediately, overriding any previously stated abstract commitments to long-term sobriety.
**Semantic Justification:** Because the individual's [[Temporal_Horizon|temporal horizon]] is biologically truncated to 9 days, distal outcomes like health or employment are computationally invisible and possess a subjective value of zero. Therefore, choosing the immediate, high-magnitude pharmacological reward to alleviate localized craving is the only economically rational choice within their restricted perceptual field.
