# Overpowering Localized Craving

## Emotion Frame Elements
* **Experiencer:** [[Addicted_Individual|Individual with severe substance addiction]]
* **Trigger:** Environmental cues that predict the availability of the drug
* **Appraisal:** The immediate drug is the only economically rational choice; distal rewards have a subjective value of zero.
* **Physiological Substrate:** Intensely conditioned anticipatory phasic dopamine release within the dorsal striatum and basolateral amygdala.
* **Social Display Rule:** Often expressed as sudden, seemingly irrational abandonment of social commitments and recovery goals to acquire the drug.
