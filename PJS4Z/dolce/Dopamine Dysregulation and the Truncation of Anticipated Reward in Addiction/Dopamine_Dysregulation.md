# Dopamine Dysregulation

**Type:** Physical Entity

**Description:** The severe neurobiological adaptation characterized by D2 receptor downregulation, altered Reward Prediction Error (RPE) signals, and frontostriatal circuit failure. This is the micro-intrinsic substrate that drives the truncation of the [[Temporal_Horizon|temporal horizon]].
