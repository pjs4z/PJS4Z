# Natural Non-Drug Rewards

**Objective Quality:** Blunted Reward Prediction Error (RPE) signals and massive downregulation of striatal D2 receptors.
**Subjective Quale:** Profound subjective anhedonia; a complete loss of interest in and inability to 'look forward to' previously anticipated hobbies or social interactions. *(See: [[vibe_subjective_anhedonia|Subjective Anhedonia towards Natural Rewards]])*
