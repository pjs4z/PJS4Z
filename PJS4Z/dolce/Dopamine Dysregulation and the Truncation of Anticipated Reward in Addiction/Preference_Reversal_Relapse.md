# Preference Reversal (Relapse)

## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
* **Pre-State:** An individual in recovery maintains a significant temporal distance from the drug, valuing the Larger-Later Reward (LLR) of sustained abstinence and a healthy life.
* **Execution:** The temporal distance to the drug opportunity closes, causing the subjective value of the immediate drug (Smaller-Sooner Reward) to skyrocket hyperbolically, intercepting and eclipsing the discounted LLR.
* **Post-State:** The individual abandons the long-term goal and consumes the drug, executing a mathematically predictable relapse driven by the truncated [[Temporal_Horizon|temporal horizon]].
