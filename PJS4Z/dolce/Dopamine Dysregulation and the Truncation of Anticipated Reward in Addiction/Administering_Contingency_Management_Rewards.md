# Administering Contingency Management Rewards

**Agent:** [[Clinical_Therapist|Clinical Therapist]]
**Is Caused By Intention:** To provide immediate, concrete reinforcement (vouchers) directly within the patient's functional temporal window to alter their choice architecture.
**Semantic Justification:** The therapist recognizes that the patient's brain is biologically incapable of valuing distant abstract futures (e.g., 4.7 years) due to a 9-day [[Temporal_Horizon|temporal horizon]]. Providing immediate tangible rewards for verified abstinence is the only rational, mathematically viable way to outcompete the immediate drug cue and stabilize the patient's neurobiology.
