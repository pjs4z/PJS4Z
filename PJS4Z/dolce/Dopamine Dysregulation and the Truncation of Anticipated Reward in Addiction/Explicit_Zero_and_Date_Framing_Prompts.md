# Explicit-Zero and Date Framing Prompts

**Causal Force:** Semantically alters the conceptual framing of time and reward (e.g., explicitly writing '$0 later' or specific calendar dates), forcing the reader's cognitive attention forward out of the present moment and significantly reducing impulsive choice without requiring additional willpower.
**Constrains/Empowers:** Test Subjects, Impulsive Individuals
