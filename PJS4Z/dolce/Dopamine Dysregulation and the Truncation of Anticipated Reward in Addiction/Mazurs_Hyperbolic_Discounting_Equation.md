# Mazur's Hyperbolic Discounting Equation

**Causal Force:** Mathematically defines and predicts the subjective devaluation of delayed rewards, transforming the clinical phenomenon of 'relapse' from a moral failure into a predictable mathematical outcome of a steep discounting curve intercepting a proximal reward.
**Constrains/Empowers:** Behavioral Economists, [[Addicted_Individual|Addicted Individuals]]
