# Addicted Individual

## BDI Triad (Target Action: [[Consuming_Immediate_Drug_Relapse|Consuming the immediate drug of abuse (Relapse)]])
* **Belief:** Distal outcomes (health, employment, family) are computationally invisible and possess a subjective value of zero; therefore, the immediate drug is the only economically rational choice available.
* **Desire:** To achieve immediate, high-magnitude pharmacological reinforcement and alleviate the overpowering surge in localized craving triggered by environmental cues.
* **Intention:** To acquire and consume the drug immediately, overriding any previously stated abstract commitments to long-term sobriety.

## Social Moments
* Fracturing of family relationships
* Loss of stable long-term employment
* Breaking of social and therapeutic commitments to abstinence
* Potential legal incarceration
