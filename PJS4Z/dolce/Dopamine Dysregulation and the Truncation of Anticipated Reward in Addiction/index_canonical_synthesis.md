# The Truncation of the Temporal Horizon and Preference Reversal in Addiction

**Summary:** Chronic drug exposure induces micro-intrinsic [[Dopamine_Dysregulation|dopamine dysregulation]] that violently truncates the [[Temporal_Horizon|temporal horizon]], fundamentally altering the meso-cognitive plane by rendering distal rewards computationally invisible and driving [[vibe_overpowering_localized_craving|overpowering immediate craving]]. This internal collapse of future-oriented intentionality forces the macro-contextual plane to adapt, necessitating inscribed environmental prostheses like [[Contingency_Management_CM|Contingency Management]] vouchers to artificially bridge the temporal gap and prevent systemic social fallout.

## The Tripartite Convergence Matrix

> [!info] The Micro-Intrinsic Plane (Substrate)
> **Somatic/Systemic Drivers:**
> * Massive downregulation of striatal dopamine D2 receptors.
> * Blunted Reward Prediction Error (RPE) signals for [[Natural_Non_Drug_Rewards|natural rewards]].
> * Hyper-sensitized anticipatory phasic dopamine release in the dorsal striatum and basolateral amygdala.
> * Hypometabolism and severed functional connectivity in the ventromedial prefrontal cortex (vmPFC).
> 
> **Conceptual Space Coordinates:**
> * Temporal_Horizon_Breadth: 0.05
> * Delayed_Reward_Utility: 0.05
> * Natural_Reward_Sensitivity: 0.1
> * Executive_Future_Coupling: 0.1
> *(See: [[Severe_Substance_Addiction_Cohort|Severe Substance Addiction Cohort]])*

> [!warning] The Meso-Cognitive Plane (Internal/Agentive)
> **Belief-Desire-Intention (BDI):**
> * **Belief:** Distal outcomes (health, employment) are computationally invisible and possess a subjective value of zero.
> * **Desire:** To achieve immediate, high-magnitude pharmacological reinforcement and alleviate overpowering localized craving.
> * **Intention:** To acquire and consume the drug immediately, overriding previously stated abstract commitments to long-term sobriety.
> *(See: [[Addicted_Individual|Addicted Individual Agent Profile]])*
> 
> **Emotion Situation (Vibe):**
> * [[vibe_overpowering_localized_craving|Overpowering Localized Craving]] coupled with [[vibe_subjective_anhedonia|Subjective Anhedonia towards Natural Rewards]].
> 
> **Semantic Justification:**
> Because the individual's [[Temporal_Horizon|temporal horizon]] is biologically truncated to 9 days, distal outcomes are computationally invisible; therefore, choosing the immediate, high-magnitude pharmacological reward is the only economically rational choice within their restricted perceptual field. *(See: [[Consuming_Immediate_Drug_Relapse|Action: Consuming Immediate Drug]])*

> [!abstract] The Macro-Contextual Plane (External/Normative)
> **Normative Descriptions (D&S):**
> * [[ds_tdrl_and_rpe|Temporal Difference Reinforcement Learning (TDRL)]] framework mathematically distorting learning rates to heavily favor recency.
> * [[Mazurs_Hyperbolic_Discounting_Equation|Mazur's Hyperbolic Discounting Equation]] defining the steep subjective devaluation of delayed rewards.
> 
> **Inscribed Acts & Constraints:**
> * [[Verified_Urinalysis_and_Financial_Voucher_Records|Verified Urinalysis and Financial Voucher Records]] acting as an immediate environmental prosthesis.
> * [[Explicit_Zero_and_Date_Framing_Prompts|Explicit-Zero and Date Framing Prompts]] semantically altering the conceptual framing of time and reward.
> 
> **Social Dependencies:**
> * Fracturing of family relationships.
> * Loss of stable long-term employment.
> * Breaking of social and therapeutic commitments to abstinence.
> * Potential legal incarceration.
