# Narrative Completion Paradigm (Petry, Bickel, Arnett)

**Generated Situation:** Heroin-dependent subjects generated future narratives averaging only 9 days from the present, whereas healthy controls projected events 4.7 years into the future.
**Perspectival Bias:** Intentionally disregarding the qualitative, thematic, or moral content of the narratives to extract a pure, uncontaminated metric of spontaneous temporal projection.
