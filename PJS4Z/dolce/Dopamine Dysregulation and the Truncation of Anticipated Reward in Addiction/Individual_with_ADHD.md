# Individual with ADHD

## BDI Triad (Target Action: [[Aborting_Delayed_Reward_Task|Aborting a delayed-reward task to seek immediate stimulation]])
* **Belief:** The unbridged temporal gap before the reward is delivered is psychologically painful, under-stimulated, and behaviorally unsustainable.
* **Desire:** To escape the acute, biologically driven subjective distress of waiting. *(See: [[vibe_acute_delay_aversion|Acute Delay Aversion]])*
* **Intention:** To abandon the delayed goal and immediately seek alternative environmental sensory stimulation.

## Social Moments
* Non-compliance with experimental or social expectations of patience
* Disruption of structured social or academic environments
