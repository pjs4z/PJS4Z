# Trans-Disease Model of Anticipatory Dysfunction

**Generated Situation:** Diverse conditions such as ADHD, clinical obesity, compulsive sexual behavior, and parasuicidality exhibit the same underlying truncation of the [[Temporal_Horizon|temporal horizon]] and [[Delay_Discounting|delay discounting]] as substance abuse.
**Perspectival Bias:** Elevating temporal horizon truncation from a secondary symptom of drug abuse to a primary, universal behavioral marker governing a broad spectrum of human psychiatric disorders.
