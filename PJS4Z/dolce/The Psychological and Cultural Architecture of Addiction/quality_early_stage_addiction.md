# Qualities of Early Stage Addiction
**Target Entity:** Early Stage Addiction
* **Objective Quality:** Midbrain limbic dopamine system activation and positive reinforcement.
* **Subjective Quale:** Chemically induced euphoria, boundless energy, and the illusion of compartmentalized control.
