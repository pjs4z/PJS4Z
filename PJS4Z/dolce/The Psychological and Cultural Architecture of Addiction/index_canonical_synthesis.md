# The systemic, progressive collapse of the self and the surrounding social network due to addiction.

**Summary:** The neurobiological hijacking of the prefrontal limbic circuit forces the agent to rationalize profound moral compromises and endure solipsistic despair. This internal collapse inevitably shatters the codependent family system and triggers strict institutional and legal accountability.

**Event Description:** The multidimensional trajectory of addiction in Western narrative media, characterized by the neurobiological deterioration of [[executive_function|Executive Function]], the erosion of interpersonal morality, and a descent into profound emotional dysphoria, which simultaneously inflicts severe collateral damage on codependent family systems.

> **Somatic/Systemic Drivers:**
> * Midbrain limbic dopamine system activation and positive reinforcement.
> * Prefrontal limbic circuit dysfunction.
> * Severe physical withdrawal and delirium tremens.
> * Chemical blunting of the nervous system.

> * **Desire:** To achieve positive reinforcement, numb painful self-assessment, or stave off agonizing withdrawal.
> * **Intention:** To manipulate social networks, deceive loved ones, and subordinate all actions to substance pursuit.
