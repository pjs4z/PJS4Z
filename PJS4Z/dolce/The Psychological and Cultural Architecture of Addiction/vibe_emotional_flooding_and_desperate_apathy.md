# Emotional Flooding and Desperate Apathy
## Emotion Frame Elements
* **Experiencer:** Dual-diagnosis addicts (e.g., Rue Bennett in Euphoria)
* **Trigger:** Overwhelming emotional flooding from co-occurring mental health disorders (OCD, ADHD, bipolar symptoms, anxiety)
* **Appraisal:** Viewing life as inherently tragic and meaningless; a profound lack of self-love and acceptance of premature death
* **Physiological Substrate:** Extreme emotional volatility, hyper-awareness, and the chemical blunting of the nervous system to reach a baseline of apathy
* **Social Display Rule:** Self-isolation, moodiness, and the rejection of normative adolescent social expectations
