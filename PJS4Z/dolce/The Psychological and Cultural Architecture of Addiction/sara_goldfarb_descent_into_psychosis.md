# Sara Goldfarb's Descent Into Psychosis
## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
* **Pre-State:** Possessing a culturally endorsed goal to lose weight to fit into a red dress for a television appearance.
* **Execution:** Consuming prescription amphetamine-based diet pills to achieve positive reinforcement and boundless energy.
* **Post-State:** Experiencing devastating hallucinations, complete breakdown of executive reality testing, irreversible psychosis, and institutionalization.
