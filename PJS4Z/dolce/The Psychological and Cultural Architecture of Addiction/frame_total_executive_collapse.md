# Total Executive Collapse Frame
**Authorial Perspective:** Deterministic and horrifying; aligns with the strict [[disease_model|disease model]] depicting the absolute hijacking of the brain's decision-making apparatus.

## Frame Elements
* **Agent:** The Late-Stage Addict (e.g., [[agent_don_birnam|Don Birnam]], [[agent_sara_goldfarb|Sara Goldfarb]])
* **Target:** The addictive substance (e.g., alcohol, amphetamine-based diet pills)
* **Trigger:** The primal demands of the midbrain limbic dopamine system and negative emotional cycles of withdrawal
* **Prior State:** Possessing culturally endorsed goals or basic societal competence
* **Outcome:** Complete breakdown of reality testing, irreversible psychosis, institutionalization, and subordination of all action to substance pursuit

**Missing Variables:** Conscious choice or moral agency in the late stages of the disease
