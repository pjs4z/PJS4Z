# Wegscheider-Cruse Family Systems Theory
**Generated Situation:** Family members unconsciously adopt rigid defense roles ([[caretaker_enabler|Caretaker]], Hero, Scapegoat) to absorb the shock of the addiction and maintain a facade of normalcy.
**Perspectival Bias:** Systemic and relational; reframes addiction from a solitary individual failing to a highly destructive, codependent 'family disease' where collateral damage is inevitable.
